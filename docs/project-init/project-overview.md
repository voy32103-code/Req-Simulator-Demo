# Project Overview

Req Simulator is an AI-powered requirement gathering training platform for IT students, software engineering students, information systems students, junior developers, and career switchers.

The product helps learners practice a hard real-world skill: interviewing stakeholders and turning incomplete conversations into clear requirements.

## Startup Proposal Fit

- Practical: targets a clear education and workforce training pain point.
- Creative: uses AI as realistic stakeholders, not only as a tutor.
- AI-first: stakeholder simulation, hidden requirement gating, gap detection, and feedback are central.
- Feasible: starts with a narrow MVP and expands toward B2B SaaS.
- Business model: subscriptions for universities, bootcamps, training centers, and companies.
