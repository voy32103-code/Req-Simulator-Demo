# Project Requirements

## Product Goal

Build an AI-first requirement gathering training platform where learners practice interviewing simulated stakeholders and receive structured feedback.

## MVP User Goals

- Students can choose a realistic business scenario.
- Students can interview an AI-simulated stakeholder.
- Students can take notes during the interview.
- Students can submit user stories, use cases, and acceptance criteria.
- Students can receive AI-style scoring and feedback.
- Instructors can view sessions, scores, feedback, gaps, and add review comments.

## First Demo Scenario

University Course Registration System.

## Core Differentiator

Req Simulator is a structured simulation environment with hidden requirements, scoring, and instructor review. It is not a generic chatbot.
